Riddle Puzzle

You and your team are exhausted, but after a full day of diving deep into the ocean, you have finally reached the mysterious cave. You quickly throw on your scuba gear and carefully exit the submarine. Reluctantly, you all swim up to the crack you thought might be a door. The week leading up to the exploratory dive, your team had pondered ideas of how to determine if this crack was in fact a door. After careful consideration, you all decided to do just what the detective in all of your favorite action movies chose to do when finding a trap door, try to press on the wall to see if it opens. Sure enough, when your teammate does just that, the wall beings to open revealing a dark room. As you swim in, the cave door closes behind you and the water suddenly begins to drain. The next thing you know, the dry room is lit! But all you can see is a distressed and aged piece of paper on the ground. After a few minutes of sheer shock, confusion, and utter disbelief, you all remove your mouthpiece, oxygen, goggles, and flippers. You pick up the piece of paper and read-

It's a riddle! You spot another door on the opposite end of the room and decide to check it out. You see a letter keypad on the door and figure the riddle must give you the password needed to open it.
